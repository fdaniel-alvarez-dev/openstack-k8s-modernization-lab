# Runbook: Capacity & Cost Review (Quarterly)

## Goals
- Keep performance and reliability while preventing waste.

## Inputs
- Resource utilization trends (compute, storage, network).
- Tenant growth and workload forecasts.
- Incident patterns related to capacity (saturation, queueing, throttling).

## Outputs
- Capacity plan (add/remove nodes, rebalance, tune overcommit).
- Cost actions: right-sizing, storage tiering, and automation to reduce toil.
